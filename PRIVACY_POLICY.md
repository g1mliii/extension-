# Privacy Policy - foglite URL Rating Extension

**Last Updated:** January 31, 2025

## Overview

foglite is a community-driven URL trust rating extension that helps users make informed browsing decisions by providing trust scores and community ratings for websites. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## Information We Collect

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Current Page URL**: We collect the URL of the webpage you're currently viewing to provide trust scores and ratings
- **Usage Analytics**: Basic extension usage patterns (anonymized)
- **Technical Information**: Browser type, extension version, and error logs for debugging purposes

### User-Provided Information (Optional)
- **Account Information**: Email address and encrypted password if you choose to create an account
- **Ratings and Reviews**: Star ratings (1-5) and flags (spam, misleading, scam) you submit for websites
- **Affiliate Interactions**: Click tracking for affiliate links (stored locally only)

## How We Use Your Information

### Core Functionality
- **Trust Score Calculation**: URLs are used to calculate and display community trust scores
- **Rating Aggregation**: User ratings are combined to create overall trust scores for websites
- **Community Protection**: Flagged content helps protect other users from harmful websites

### Service Improvement
- **Error Handling**: Technical information helps us fix bugs and improve performance
- **Feature Development**: Usage patterns help us understand which features are most valuable

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage
- **Cache Data**: Trust scores and ratings are cached locally for 5 minutes to improve performance
- **User Preferences**: Extension settings and preferences stored in browser storage
- **Affiliate Tracking**: Click tracking data stored locally only (never transmitted)

### Remote Storage (Supabase)
- **User Accounts**: Encrypted authentication data stored securely
- **Community Ratings**: Aggregated ratings and trust scores (no personal identification)
- **Domain Analysis**: Technical security data for websites (SSL status, domain age, etc.)

### Security Measures
- **Encryption**: All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
- **Authentication**: Secure JWT token-based authentication system
- **Access Control**: Row-level security policies protect user data
- **No Sensitive Data**: We never store passwords in plain text or collect sensitive personal information

## Data Sharing and Third Parties

### What We Don't Share
- **Personal Information**: We never sell or share personal information with third parties
- **Individual Ratings**: Your specific ratings are not shared with other users
- **Browsing History**: We don't track or store your browsing history

### Third-Party Services
- **Supabase**: Database and authentication services (privacy-compliant)
- **Security APIs**: Domain security checks using Google Safe Browsing and similar services
- **Affiliate Programs**: 1Password and NordVPN (click tracking only, no personal data shared)

### Aggregated Data
- **Community Trust Scores**: Anonymized, aggregated ratings are shared to calculate trust scores
- **Security Intelligence**: Anonymized threat data may be used to improve community protection

## Your Rights and Choices

### Account Control
- **Account Deletion**: You can delete your account at any time
- **Data Export**: Request a copy of your data (ratings and account information)
- **Rating Management**: Edit or delete your ratings for any website

### Privacy Controls
- **Anonymous Usage**: You can use the extension without creating an account
- **Opt-Out**: Disable specific features like affiliate link tracking
- **Data Clearing**: Clear local cache and stored data through browser settings

## Affiliate Links and Monetization

### Affiliate Partnerships
- **Current Partners**: 1Password (password manager) and NordVPN (VPN service)
- **Tracking**: We track clicks on affiliate links for commission purposes only
- **No Personal Data**: Affiliate tracking uses anonymous identifiers only
- **Local Storage**: Click tracking data is stored locally and not transmitted to third parties

### Revenue Model
- **Affiliate Commissions**: We earn commissions from affiliate partner sales
- **No Data Sales**: We never sell user data or personal information
- **Transparent Disclosure**: All affiliate relationships are clearly disclosed

## Children's Privacy

foglite is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

## International Users

foglite is operated from the United States. If you are located outside the United States, please be aware that information we collect will be transferred to and processed in the United States.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by:
- Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
- Displaying a notification in the extension for significant changes
- Sending email notifications to registered users for major policy changes

## Data Retention

- **Account Data**: Retained until account deletion
- **Ratings**: Retained indefinitely as part of community trust scores (anonymized)
- **Cache Data**: Automatically cleared after 5 minutes (local) or 7 days (domain analysis)
- **Logs**: Error logs retained for 30 days for debugging purposes
